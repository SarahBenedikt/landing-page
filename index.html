Process Mapping
& Optimisation

I transform chaotic workflows into clear, repeatable systems. Using BPMN models, I document your processes end-to-end—capturing system touchpoints, data flows, roles, and handover points. You get actionable SOPs that eliminate guesswork and improve both data quality and team coordination.

Data Analysis
& Strategy

I turn your data into decisions. By defining KPIs tied to real business outcomes, analyzing operational patterns, and surfacing hidden insights—whether it's seasonal demand, engagement drop-off, or bottleneck causes—I help you move from gut feelings to evidence-based action.

Workflow Optimisation
& Smart Automation

Not everything needs automation. Sometimes a clearer process is all it takes. I redesign workflows to eliminate confusion, then automate only what's truly repetitive. Operations chatbots, validation checks, no-code database queries. The outcome: your team spends less time on mechanics and more on decisions that move the business forward.
