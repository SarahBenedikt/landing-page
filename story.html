My Story

From logistics to digital process design

Where It Started

I started out in logistics, where I learned to visualize and optimize complex systems. Coordinating shipments, managing workflows, and solving operational puzzles taught me how to see the bigger picture while keeping track of every detail.

This foundation now fuels how I build digital process structures. The skills I developed in physical logistics translate directly into designing efficient digital workflows.

Cross-Functional Experience

Having worked across logistics, sales, process management, and IT, I understand how every part of a business connects — from day-to-day operations to strategic and technical decisions.

This breadth of experience means I can speak the language of different teams, bridge gaps between departments, and design solutions that work for everyone involved.

My Approach

I combine structure and creativity, translating complex, human workflows into clear, scalable systems. I believe that good processes should feel natural — they should support people, not constrain them.

Whether it's mapping a workflow, defining KPIs, or building an automation, my goal is always the same: create clarity, reduce friction, and help teams focus on what matters most.

Let's Connect

If you're looking to bring structure to your operations, make sense of your data, or explore how automation could help your business — I'd love to hear from you.
